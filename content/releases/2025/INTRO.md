# Arborito 2025: Next-Gen Infrastructure (BETA) 🚀

This is the **Preliminary 2025 Release**. It represents the ongoing effort to modernize the Arborito curriculum with a focus on high-scale systems and cloud native technologies.

## 🏗️ Current Focus: Modern Infrastructure

The 2025 syllabus marks a transition from desktop-centric learning to infrastructure-as-code:

*   **Modern Linux:** Focusing on the 6.x Kernel series and performance optimization.
*   **Cloud & Containers:** Early drafts on Docker, Podman, and orchestration.
*   **Modern Automation:** Shifting from simple Bash to advanced scripting and CI/CD concepts.
